Community Code of Conduct

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

Building a Supportive Founder Community

Welcome to Our Community!

The Founders Monday Global community is built on mutual respect, collaboration, and support. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all community members, including founders, mentors, investors, and supporters.

Our goal is to create a safe, inclusive, and productive environment where startup founders can connect, learn, and grow together.

Our Core Values

Respect

Treat all members with dignity and respect, regardless of background, experience, or startup stage.

Collaboration

Share knowledge, resources, and experiences to help each other succeed.

Integrity

Be honest, transparent, and ethical in all interactions and dealings.

Inclusion

Welcome and support founders from all backgrounds, industries, and locations.

Growth

Focus on learning, improvement, and helping each other achieve milestones.

Global Mindset

Embrace diverse perspectives and think beyond local boundaries.

Expected Behavior

Be Supportive

Offer constructive feedback, celebrate others' successes, and provide help when possible.

Share Knowledge

Contribute valuable insights, resources, and experiences to help fellow founders.

Maintain Confidentiality

Respect the privacy of shared information and protect sensitive startup details.

Give Credit

Acknowledge sources and give proper attribution when sharing others' work.

Be Professional

Communicate clearly, meet commitments, and handle disagreements respectfully.

Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated in our community:

Harassment & Discrimination

  • Offensive comments related to gender, race, ethnicity, religion, disability, sexual orientation, or age
  • Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
  • Bullying, intimidation, or personal attacks
  • Discriminatory jokes or language

Professional Misconduct

  • Sharing confidential information without permission
  • Plagiarism or intellectual property theft
  • Misrepresentation of credentials or achievements
  • Spam, self-promotion, or commercial solicitation without consent

Community Disruption

  • Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
  • Spreading misinformation or false claims
  • Excessive arguments or drama
  • Creating multiple accounts to bypass restrictions

Legal Violations

  • Sharing illegal content or encouraging illegal activities
  • Violating intellectual property rights
  • Sharing malicious software or links
  • Fraudulent activities or scams

Platform-Specific Guidelines

WhatsApp Group

  • Keep discussions relevant to startups and entrepreneurship
  • Avoid forwarding irrelevant messages or chain messages
  • Respect time zones when sending messages
  • Use appropriate language and tone
  • No spamming or excessive self-promotion

Discussion Forum

  • Post in appropriate categories
  • Search before creating new topics
  • Provide helpful, detailed responses
  • Use clear, descriptive titles
  • Report inappropriate content

Events & Meetups

  • RSVP responsibly
  • Arrive on time or notify if running late
  • Participate actively and respectfully
  • Follow event-specific rules
  • Network professionally

Reporting & Enforcement

1

Report Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it immediately to:

  • Email: conduct@foundersmonday.com
  • Community Managers: Direct message on platform
  • Anonymous Reporting: Available through our website
2

Investigation

Our team will:

  • Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
  • Investigate confidentially
  • Contact involved parties if needed
  • Review evidence and context
3

Action

Based on investigation, we may:

  • Issue a warning
  • Require an apology
  • Temporarily suspend account
  • Permanently ban from community
  • Report to authorities if illegal

Protection for Reporters

We will protect reporters from retaliation. All reports are handled confidentially, and we will not disclose reporter identities without permission.

Founder Spotlight Guidelines

Application Process

  • Provide accurate and complete information
  • Only submit your own startup/work
  • Disclose any conflicts of interest
  • Be available for follow-up interviews

Prize Acceptance

  • Use prize money responsibly for your startup
  • Provide feedback on how the prize helped
  • Share your success story with the community
  • Pay any applicable taxes

Featured Founder Responsibilities

  • Engage with community questions
  • Share insights and lessons learned
  • Support other founders
  • Represent the community positively

Mentorship Guidelines

For Mentors

  • Provide guidance, not decisions
  • Respect mentee confidentiality
  • Set clear boundaries and expectations
  • Be honest about your expertise limits
  • Do not solicit business inappropriately

For Mentees

  • Respect mentor time and expertise
  • Come prepared to sessions
  • Implement advice and provide feedback
  • Don't expect immediate solutions
  • Express gratitude for guidance

Confidentiality & Intellectual Property

Protect Sensitive Information

Do not share confidential information about your startup or others' startups without explicit permission.

Respect IP Rights

Do not share copyrighted material without permission. Give proper attribution when sharing others' work.

Non-Disclosure

When discussing sensitive topics, consider using NDAs. Our platform does not provide legal protection for shared ideas.

Global Community Considerations

Cultural Sensitivity

Be aware of cultural differences in communication styles, business practices, and social norms.

Time Zone Awareness

Consider time zones when scheduling meetings or expecting responses.

Language & Communication

Use clear, simple language. Be patient with non-native English speakers.

Legal Differences

Understand that laws and regulations vary by country. Seek local legal advice when needed.

Our Commitment

We Are Committed To:

  • Maintaining a safe, inclusive environment
  • Enforcing this Code of Conduct consistently
  • Continuously improving our community
  • Listening to member feedback
  • Leading by example
