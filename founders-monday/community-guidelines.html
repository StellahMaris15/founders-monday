Community Guidelines

Our commitment to maintaining a respectful, supportive, and valuable community for all startup founders.

Introduction Our Values Communication Prohibited Reporting

Introduction & Purpose

Welcome to the Founders Monday Global community! These guidelines are designed to ensure our community remains a safe, supportive, and valuable space for all members. By participating in our community, you agree to follow these guidelines.

Our Purpose

We exist to empower startup founders through connection, knowledge sharing, and mutual support. Our community thrives when all members contribute positively and respectfully.

Community Commitment

Every member of Founders Monday Global agrees to:

  • Treat all members with respect and dignity
  • Contribute positively to discussions
  • Share knowledge and experiences openly
  • Support fellow founders in their journey
  • Report any violations of these guidelines

Our Core Values

These values guide everything we do in our community

Respect

We value diverse perspectives and treat everyone with dignity, regardless of background, experience, or startup stage.

Knowledge Sharing

We believe in sharing insights, experiences, and lessons learned to help each other grow and succeed.

Collaboration

We support and uplift each other, celebrating successes and offering help during challenges.

Excellence

We strive for quality in everything we do, from discussions to events to support offered.

Inclusion

We welcome founders from all backgrounds, industries, and regions to join our global community.

Integrity

We act with honesty and transparency in all our interactions and community activities.

Communication Guidelines

How we communicate and interact within our community

Do's

Positive behaviors we encourage

Introduce Yourself

Share your background, startup, and what you're looking to gain from the community when you join.

Be Constructive

Offer constructive feedback and suggestions that help others improve and grow.

Offer Help

Share your expertise and help others when you have relevant knowledge or experience.

Search First

Check if your question has already been answered before posting a new one.

Give Credit

Acknowledge sources and give credit when sharing others' ideas or content.

Don'ts

Behaviors to avoid

No Harassment

Never engage in harassment, bullying, or personal attacks against other members.

No Spamming

Avoid excessive self-promotion, irrelevant links, or repeated posting of the same content.

No Private Info

Never share private information about yourself or others without explicit consent.

No Hate Speech

Do not post content that promotes hate, discrimination, or violence against any group.

No Plagiarism

Do not present others' work as your own. Always cite sources properly.

Prohibited Content & Activities

Content and activities that are strictly prohibited in our community

Zero Tolerance

The following activities will result in immediate removal from the community without warning:

Illegal Activities

Promotion or discussion of illegal activities, including fraud, scams, or harassment.

Hate Speech

Content that attacks or demeans individuals or groups based on race, gender, religion, etc.

Privacy Violations

Sharing private information about others without consent (doxing).

Intellectual Property

Sharing copyrighted material without permission or proper attribution.

Malicious Content

Posting viruses, malware, or links to harmful websites.

Impersonation

Pretending to be someone else or creating fake accounts.

Disruption

Intentionally disrupting conversations or community activities.

Unauthorized Sales

Selling products or services without prior approval from community admins.

Reporting & Enforcement

How to report violations and how we enforce these guidelines

1

Report Violations

If you see a violation of these guidelines, report it immediately using the report button or by contacting our moderation team.

Email Moderation Team
2

Review Process

Our moderation team will review the report within 24 hours. All reports are confidential.

3

Take Action

Depending on the severity, actions may include warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans.

4

Appeal Process

Members may appeal decisions by contacting our team. Appeals are reviewed by senior moderators.

Enforcement Guidelines

Warning Minor Violations

First-time minor violations typically result in a warning and educational resources.

Temporary Suspension Repeated Violations

Repeated violations may result in temporary suspension from community activities.

Permanent Ban Severe Violations

Severe violations or harassment result in immediate and permanent removal.
