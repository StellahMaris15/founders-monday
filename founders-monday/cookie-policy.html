Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

How We Use Cookies

Founders Monday Global uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality, such as page navigation and access to secure areas.

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and choices, such as language preferences and accessibility settings.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Marketing Cookies

Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept or reject non-essential cookies. Essential cookies cannot be rejected as they are necessary for the website to function properly.

How to Manage Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by:

  • Clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer
  • Using our cookie consent manager
  • Adjusting your browser settings

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu.

Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, you can manage cookies through your browser settings or device settings.

Opt-Out Tools

You can use tools like the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page or Your Online Choices.

What Happens If You Disable Cookies?

If you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly:

You may need to re-enter information each time you visit

Personalized features may not work correctly

Shopping cart functionality may be impaired

We won't be able to analyze how you use our website

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
